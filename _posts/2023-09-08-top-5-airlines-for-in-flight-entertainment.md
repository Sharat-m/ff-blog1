Air travel is often a necessary, but not always enjoyable, part of life. However, with the advent of in-flight entertainment systems, flying has become much more bearable. Whether you're looking to catch up on a TV show, watch a movie, or simply listen to some music, the right in-flight entertainment can make all the difference. That's why we've compiled a list of the top 5 airlines for in-flight entertainment.

^TOC^

## Emirates
![emirates]({{'posts/2023-09-08-top-5-airlines-for-in-flight-entertainment/emirates.png' | asset:'@path'}})
Emirates is known for its luxurious amenities, and in-flight entertainment is no exception. With over 4,500 channels of movies, TV shows, music, and games, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Passengers can watch the latest Hollywood blockbusters or catch up on their favorite TV shows. The airline also offers live TV, so you can keep up with news and sports while in the air. The icing on the cake? Emirates' ice system, which allows passengers to browse content on their own personal screens or via the airline's app.

##  Qatar Airways
![qatar-airways]({{'posts/2023-09-08-top-5-airlines-for-in-flight-entertainment/qatar-airways.png' | asset:'@path'}})
Qatar Airways is another airline that offers a top-notch in-flight entertainment system. The airline's Oryx One system features over 4,000 options, including movies, TV shows, games, and music. Passengers can also access live TV, as well as a selection of international newspapers and magazines. The system is available on all of the airline's long-haul flights, and passengers can even create a playlist of their favorite songs and access it on future flights.

##  Virgin Atlantic
![virgin-atlantic]({{'posts/2023-09-08-top-5-airlines-for-in-flight-entertainment/virgin-atlantic.png' | asset:'@path'}})
Virgin Atlantic is known for its innovative approach to travel, and its in-flight entertainment system is no exception. The airline's Vera system offers a wide selection of movies, TV shows, and games, as well as music and podcasts. Passengers can also access live TV and even participate in a pub quiz competition with other passengers on board. The system is available on all of the airline's long-haul flights, and passengers can create a watchlist of their favorite content to enjoy on future flights.
 
##  Singapore Airlines
![singapore-airlines]({{'posts/2023-09-08-top-5-airlines-for-in-flight-entertainment/singapore-airlines.png' | asset:'@path'}})
Singapore Airlines has long been known for its excellent service, and its in-flight entertainment system is no exception. The airline's KrisWorld system offers a wide selection of movies, TV shows, and games, as well as music and podcasts. Passengers can also access live TV, and the system is available on all of the airline's long-haul flights. Additionally, Singapore Airlines offers in-flight Wi-Fi, so passengers can stay connected even when they're in the air.

##  Delta Air Lines 
![delta-air-lines]({{'posts/2023-09-08-top-5-airlines-for-in-flight-entertainment/delta-air-lines.png' | asset:'@path'}})
Delta Air Lines is a U.S.-based airline that offers a robust in-flight entertainment system. The airline's Delta Studio system offers a wide selection of movies, TV shows, and games, as well as music and podcasts. Passengers can also access live TV, and the system is available on all of the airline's long-haul flights. Delta also offers in-flight Wi-Fi, so passengers can stay connected during their journey.

**If you're looking for an airline with a great in-flight entertainment system, you can't go wrong with any of these five options. From Emirates' luxurious ice system to Delta's robust Delta Studio, there's something for everyone to enjoy. So sit back, relax, and enjoy your flight.**
